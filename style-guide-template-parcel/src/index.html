Purpose/Theme

We are planning on incorporating a dark forest theme for our project. We are going to use a free to use background for the page and use colors from that background throughout the page as accent colors. We plan on adding effect such as blur effects behind the darker sections and possibly adding animations and hover effects such as slightly zooming in on sections as you hover, or animating buttons and symbols when they are interacted with. We also want a silhouette of some trees and hills on the bottom of the page that the content will scroll behind, as well as some fog or clouds floating slowly floating across the screen, We want to keep a theme of rounded edges and a clean design for the content.
